Bernie Sanders

Bernie Sanders is an American political figure who has served most recently as the United States Senator of the State of Vermont. He was born and raised in Brooklyn, New York and graduated from the University of Chicago. He is currently running for the Democratic candidacy for the 2020 presidential election.
